FAQ'S

1. What are your opening hours?

+

We’re open from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday through Friday, and from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekends. Stop by for your morning coffee or an afternoon pick-me-up!

2. Do you offer dairy-free or vegan options?

+

Yes! We offer a variety of dairy-free milk alternatives such as almond, oat, and soy milk. We also have vegan pastries and snack options on our menu.

3. Can I order online or for delivery?

+

Absolutely! You can order online for pickup through our website, and we also offer delivery through popular services like UberEats and DoorDash.

4. Do you provide catering services for events?

+

Yes, we offer coffee catering for events, both big and small. Whether it’s a corporate meeting or a private gathering, we can provide fresh coffee and pastries. Contact us for details!

5. Do you have a loyalty or rewards program?

+

We do! Sign up for our loyalty program to earn points with every purchase. After accumulating enough points, you can redeem them for free drinks, discounts, and more!
