Caffè Americano

A Caffè Americano is a coffee made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to drip coffee.

Cappuccino

A cappuccino is a classic coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and a thick layer of frothy milk foam.

Espresso

Espresso is a strong, concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Latte

A latte is a creamy coffee beverage made with espresso and steamed milk, typically topped with a light layer of foam.
